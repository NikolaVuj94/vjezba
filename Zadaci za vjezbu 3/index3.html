Menadzer prodavnice

Organisation and monitoring of all actions in the store, including operational, commercial and HR duties. Operational duties includes organisation of deliveries with external cooperations to the shooping mall. Organisation of employees for opening, alarming and exposing articles from delivery. Also monitoring of replenishment of the store respecting all commercial rules that company requires. Organisation and monitoring of hygiene in the store. Commercial duties includes monitoring of exposing new articles and collection in store, review commercial reports and position of best selling articles. Checking articles and prizes in window of the store. Checking position of collets, tables and hangers in store according to company rules. HR duties are to train, evaluate and motivate employees. Evaluating their knowledge every 3 months. Beside working with employees, store manager has to monitor and review all input and output documents, keeping a trade book and sending reports to company director regarding all topics above.

Menadzer sektora

Organization and verification of all actions in the shop, including operational, commercial and HR duties. Operational duties included monitoring of the delivery organization, monitoring of the report on sales-related supplementation, organizing the stockroom, etc. Commercial commitments included market analysis, reports on sales, monitoring of collections and quality of goods, price adjustments, and similar. HR duties were related to making daily, weekly and monthly personnel schedules, both in the Men sector and within the whole store, monitoring of training of all employees, work with potentials, interviews with candidates, etc. Communication with the shop manager and product manager from Spain on daily basis.

Komercijalista

Monitoring and producing sales reports on a daily basis, sending feedback on sales impacts and products needed to improve sales, communicating with regional commercialist and product manager from Spain. Also, I was in charge for making plans for the rotation of collections on a weekly basis, tracking the need for replenishing sold items, setting collections for windows with a manager and window dresser and training for potential salespeople, etc.
